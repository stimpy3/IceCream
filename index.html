Frequently Asked Questions

We use organic milk and cream from local dairy farms, and source many of our mix-ins from local producers when possible.

We're open daily from 11am to 12am, with extended hours until 1am on Fridays and Saturdays.

We have several nut-free flavors, but our kitchen does process nuts. We take precautions to avoid cross-contamination, but please inform our staff of any allergies.

Our ice cream is best enjoyed within 1-2 weeks of purchase when stored properly in a freezer at -18°C or below.

Our History

Scoops began in 2010 when founder NIGGA started churning small batches of ice cream in his kitchen. Inspired by his grandmother's recipes and a passion for unique flavors, NIGGA's creations quickly gained a following at local farmers' markets. In 2012, we opened our first shop downtown, focusing on seasonal ingredients and creative combinations. Today, with three locations across the city, we continue to push the boundaries of frozen treats while staying true to our small-batch, artisanal roots.
